Our Conversion Process

1. Initial Assessment

We thoroughly evaluate your classic car to determine the best conversion strategy.

2. Engine Removal

The combustion engine and related components are carefully removed.

3. Electric Motor Installation

A high-performance electric motor is installed and integrated with the car's systems.

4. Battery Integration

State-of-the-art battery packs are fitted, optimizing performance and range.

5. Electronics Update

Modern control systems and electronics are integrated for smooth operation.

6. Final Testing

Rigorous testing ensures your classic electric car meets our high standards.
